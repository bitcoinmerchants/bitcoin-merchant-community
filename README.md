# Bitcoin Merchant Community

This repo contains the code, copy and image assets that power the Bitcoin Merchant Community website. 

It is a public and open-source resource provided under an MIT License. 

## Contributing to the Bitcoin Merchant Community

We welcome feedback and contributions to the BMC website and community. 

- If you have suggestions for improving the website, please raise Github issues on this repository. 
- If you are a merchant looking to engage with other merchants, the BMC [Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/bitcoinmerchants) is the best place to do that.
- If you are a designer with a suggestion for improving the marketing assets the BMC makes available publicly, visit the [marketing-assets repo](https://github.com/bitcoinmerchants/marketing-assets/) where you can find links to design templates.
- If you are a bitcoiner wanting to connect with other bitcoiners who have been engaging local merchants about bitcoin, join the BMC Bitcoiner Google Group (COMING SOON) to discuss. 

## Criteria for bitcoin POS solutions to be included on the BMC website

The BMC seeks to include a wide variety of point-of-sale solutions that allow merchants to accept bitcoin payments irrespective of which system they currently use or where they may be locatedâ€”provided the solution can make evidence of adoption, ease of use and/or other unique value proposition. 

If you are a bitcoin POS software vendor, please [get in touch with the BMC](mailto:bitcoinmerchants@spiral.xyz) describing how your solution meets the following criteria for inclusion: 

**1. Targets bitcoin payment acceptance in the in-person retail use case**
 - Provide evidence of in-person retail merchant adoption.
 - Provide a reference/quote from at least one quote from physical retail merchant using it.

**2. Ease of set-up**
 - Explain the specific steps a merchant would need to follow to get up and running with bitcoin payments with your solution. Please provide screenshots, links to product documentation and/or video tutorials or any other materail that would help understand the getting started UX.

**3. Ease of use**
 - Describe the UX for a customer making a bitcoin payment with your solution at a merchant. Again please provide screenshots and/or any other documentation that helps illustrate the customer experience.

**4. Any other unique value proposition?**
 - Is there anything else about your solution that would qualify it as particularly notable for merchant awareness? 

**5. Assets**
 - Provide a one-line description of your POS solution in accurate, neutral language. See [existing website](https://www.bitcoinmerchantcommunity.org/) for examples.
 - Provide a high resolution logo in PNG format.
